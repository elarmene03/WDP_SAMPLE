Our Story

Girlhood Cosmetics started from a young entrepreneur who embarked on her journey at just 15 years old. Ms. Eureche Silvertino started with a dream in her heart and determination in her eyes, she opened 'Girlhood Cosmetics' - a lipgloss shop that's more than just a business. Ms. Silvertino, started this business as she was once a young girl experiencing changes in herself as she approached adoloscence. Hence, our brand name "Girlhood Cosmetics."

It's a sanctuary where every girl can embrace her journey into womanhood with confidence and style. Girlhood Cosmetics isn't just about lipgloss; it's about embracing the transformative experience of growing up. It's about celebrating the changes, the challenges, and the beauty of becoming a woman.

Eureche Silvertino's story reminds us that age is just a number when it comes to pursuing our dreams. With passion, dedication, and a sprinkle of lipgloss, anything is possible. So, here's to the dreamers, the doers, and the girls who aren't afraid to shine. Because at Girlhood Cosmetics', every shade tells a story, and every girl is celebrated.

"BIG GIRL, BIG DREAMS."
